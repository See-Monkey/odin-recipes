Potato Soup

Home

An easy and delicious creamy potato soup recipe that can be made in just one pot! Loaded with bacon and creamy potato goodness, this is a favorite comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 6 strips uncooked bacon, cut into small pieces
  • 3 tablespoons butter, unsalted or salted
  • 1 medium yellow onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  • 2.5 lbs gold potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2/3 cup heavy cream
  • 1.5 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground pepper
  • 1/4 - 1/2 teaspoon anch chile powder
  • 2/3 cup sour cream
  • shredded cheddar cheese, topping (optional)
  • fresh chives, topping, (optional)
